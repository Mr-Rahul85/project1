Just 44 km from Sasaram, near Chenari, Karamchat Dam is a beautiful hidden spot surrounded by hills. It looks like something from another world. The dam was built after the 1966 famine and sits on the Durgavati River, and that’s why it is also known as the Durgavati dam.


It is a peaceful place with stunning views, especially at sunrise and sunset when the sky reflects on the calm water. If you love nature, and photography, or just want a quiet place to relax, Karamchat Dam is perfect.


The area around the dam is full of greenery and rolling hills, making it a great place to enjoy nature. The cool breeze and gentle sound of water create a calming feeling, far from the noise of the city. If you like adventure, you can go fishing, boating, or hiking on nearby trails to explore the plants and wildlife. A boat ride on the dam is an exciting experience, and the 30-minute trip feels like it ends too soon.


Karamchat Dam is also a popular picnic spot for both locals and tourists. There is plenty of open space where families can relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings. Whether you want to take pictures, go for a hike, or just sit by the water, this place lets you connect with nature. If you ever visit Sasaram, don’t miss the chance to see this peaceful and beautiful spot.
