Just 44 km from Sasaram, near Chenari, Karamchat Dam is a beautiful hidden spot surrounded by hills. It looks like something from another world. The dam was built after the 1966 famine and sits on the Durgavati River, and that’s why it is also known as the Durgavati dam.


It is a peaceful place with stunning views, especially at sunrise and sunset when the sky reflects on the calm water. If you love nature, and photography, or just want a quiet place to relax, Karamchat Dam is perfect.
